# Primeira Parte (FP)
## Capítulo 455: Whether separated souls know that takes place on earth?

Objection 1: It would seem that separated souls know what takes place
on earth; for otherwise they would have no care for it, as they have,
according to what Dives said (Lk. 16:27,28), "I have five brethren . .
. he may testify unto them, lest they also come into the place of
torments." Therefore separated souls know what passes on earth.
Objection 2: Further, the dead often appear to the living, asleep or
awake, and tell them of what takes place there; as Samuel appeared to
Saul (1 Kings 28:11). But this could not be unless they knew what takes
place here. Therefore they know what takes place on earth.
Objection 3: Further, separated souls know what happens among
themselves. If, therefore, they do not know what takes place among us,
it must be by reason of local distance; which has been shown to be
false [745](A[7]).
