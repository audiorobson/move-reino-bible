# Suplemento (XP)
## Capítulo 198: Whether conditional consent makes a marriage?

Objection 1: It would seem that not even a conditional consent makes a
marriage, because a statement is not made simply if it is made subject
to a condition. But in marriage the words expressive of consent must be
uttered simply. Therefore a conditional consent makes no marriage.
Objection 2: Further, marriage should be certain. But where a statement
is made under a condition it is rendered doubtful. Therefore a like
consent makes no marriage.
On the contrary, In other contracts an obligation is undertaken
conditionally, and holds so long as the condition holds. Therefore
since marriage is a contract, it would seem that it can be made by a
conditional consent.
